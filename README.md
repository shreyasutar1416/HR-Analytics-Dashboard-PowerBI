# HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition trends and workforce demographics. The dashboard helps HR teams and business leaders identify key factors contributing to employee turnover and make data-driven decisions to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall employee attrition.
* Analyze attrition by age, gender, education, salary, and job role.
* Identify workforce trends and high-risk employee segments.
* Support strategic HR decision-making through visual analytics.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Total Employees:** 1470
* **Total Attrition:** 237
* **Attrition Rate:** 16.1%
* **Average Age:** 37 Years
* **Average Salary:** 6.5K
* **Average Years at Company:** 7.0

## 📊 Insights Generated

* Laboratory Technicians show the highest attrition.
* Employees aged 26–35 contribute the largest share of attrition.
* Employees earning up to 5K salary exhibit the highest turnover.
* Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds account for a significant portion of attrition.
* Male employees have a higher attrition count compared to female employees.
